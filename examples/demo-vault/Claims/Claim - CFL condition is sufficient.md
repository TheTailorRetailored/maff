# CFL condition is sufficient

For the explicit centred scheme, the condition \(r \leq 1/2\) is sufficient for stability in the Fourier-mode analysis.

## Proof routes

- Compute the amplification factor and bound its modulus over all frequencies.

## Tasks

- Check the endpoint \(r = 1/2\).
