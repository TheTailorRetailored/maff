# Research Integrity and Continuity Verification

The regression suite must prove:

- typed null-run or unassigned approvals are quarantined and cannot satisfy readiness;
- an authoring session cannot claim an independent review of its target;
- an eligible fresh session receives a locked single-use assignment;
- exact artifact access and substantive evidence sections are required;
- bare obligation ids cannot preserve proofs;
- theorem, citation, source, or PDF changes invalidate the appropriate evidence;
- an untriaged negative external review challenges the exact candidate;
- targeted major/fatal gaps block readiness;
- every completed run emits a continuation decision and an active project cannot silently lose its frontier;
- imported projects remain unverified and seed a fresh baseline audit;
- graph audits leave the project unchanged and repairs require a separate campaign;
- intermediate ingestion emits no resource link while explicit surfacing and final publication do;
- corrupted or missing managed bytes block publication.

After deployment, run a forensic/migration audit against MMRW before accepting any historical readiness state. Preserve the audit, start a fresh repair chat, and rerun exact-version gates only after its accepted findings are resolved.
